📋 Assumptions & Notes

Panel size: 400W each. System efficiency factor: 0.80 (accounts for real-world losses).

Installation cost: $3.00/watt national average (2024). Actual quotes vary ±30% by location.

Maintenance: 1% of system cost annually. IRR calculated over 25-year system life.
